Why do I need to choose an account?

HM Land Registry asks you to create an account because this data has a strict licence. This means that you can only use it in a certain way.

There are legal consequences for data misuse (using it outside the licence). For example, if you sell this data, HM Land Registry can share your account details with Royal Mail. Royal Mail may take you to court for data misuse.

The data files for account-holders all have a licence. This means that you can only use this data in a certain way. They may also have:

  • conditions of access (you need to go through security clearance before you can see the data)
  • a fee
! Warning If you do not use the data according to the licence, Royal Mail may take you to court for data misuse.

You do not need to pay anything to open an account and most of this data is also free. Read each data licence to find out how you can use the data. If you are not sure, HM Land Registry recommends that you check with Royal Mail: example@royalmail.com
