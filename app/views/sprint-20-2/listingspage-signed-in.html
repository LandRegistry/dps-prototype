Overseas companies that own property in England and Wales

CSV, 1.25Gb

Licence agreed

API available

Updated on the second working day of each month

Download data about non-UK companies that own land or property in England and Wales. The data includes (where available) company name, type of company, registration number, address and price paid. This dataset was originally called Overseas companies ownership data (OCOD).

Download data about UK companies that own land or property in England and Wales. This data includes (where available) company name, type of company, registration number, address and price paid. This data was originally called Commercial and corporate data (CCOD).

National Polygon Service

Zip file. inc. shape file & CSV, 7.3Gb

Licence agreed

API available

Updated on the second working day of each month

Download 3 datasets about registered land and property in England and Wales that show:
- indicative shape and position of boundaries
- legal interests and class of title
- unique property reference numbers


This data includes (where available) polygons, title number, estate interest, class of title, date of creation, date of update and Unique Property Reference Number (UPRN)

These datasets are free of charge and you do not need an account to access them:


1862 Act Register

Download data about leasehold properties in England and Wales.

Price Paid Data

Price Paid Data contains the sale prices of properties in England and Wales submitted to HM Land Registry for registration.

Transaction Data

Our Transaction Data includes information about the number and types of applications we have completed.

UK House Price Index: reports

The UK House Price Index (HPI) uses house sales data from HM Land Registry, Registers of Scotland, and Land and Property Services Northern Ireland and is calculated by the Office for National Statistics.

Index Polygons spatial data

Index Polygons are provided in predefined pots of data corresponding to local authority areas in England and Wales.

Geospatial Commission Data Catalogue

This catalogue contains information on some of the electronic data we hold and is based on datasets which are ‘linked to location’ through HM Land Registry title registrations.
