What happens next

Check your email. HM Land Registry will email you when your account is ready to use. You can use the link in this email to create a password. After that, you can sign in and start downloading data.

What happens if your email doesn't arrive?

Did you create a professional account for a UK organisation? HM Land Registry may take up to 2 days to approve your account

I did not get the email from HM Land Registry

    Check your junk mail folder. If you do not see the email in your junk mail:

  • Wait 2 days (if you work for a UK organisation), or

  • Email HM Land Registry: data.services@landregistry.gov.uk
