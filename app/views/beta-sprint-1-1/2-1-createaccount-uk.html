Access data on behalf of a UK organisation

To request access to the data on behalf of a UK organisation you will need to create an account. To do this you will need to:

  • Provide your name, address, email address and telephone number
  • Provide your organisation name, type and company registration number
  • Accept the terms of use for the dataset(s) you require

We will contact you within 1 working day (subject to volume of applications) to let you know the outcome of your request.
