F. Information

F.1 Details of the Information

The Information is the National Polygon Dataset and comprises the following three files:

  • Polygon File – includes all England and Wales Polygons, Title Number, Polygon ID, Polygon Status, Polygon Start/Update Date
  • Tenure File – includes Title Number, Class of Title and Tenure
  • UPRN File – includes Title Number and UPRN where available
F.2 Access to the Information

The Information will be available as follows:

  • Initial Download – being access to the National Polygon Dataset.
  • Monthly Refresh – being access to full refreshes of each file in the National Polygon Dataset every month.
  • Change Only Updates – being access to change only updates of the Polygon and Tenure files every month.
F.3 Further provisions relating to the Information

F.3.1: All Polygons supplied under this Licence are post PAI (positional accuracy improvements) shift and have variable tolerances.

F.3.2: If you intend to display the Information against Ordnance Survey or other mapping background you must have an up to date agreement or licence with Ordnance Survey or other mapping supplier permitting you to use their information to display such data. We accept no responsibility in relation to your licensing or other arrangements with Ordnance Survey or other mapping suppliers.
