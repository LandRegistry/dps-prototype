Agree to the data licences

The file you are about to download includes data owned by:

  • HM Land Registry

  • Royal Mail’s PAF® database (address data)

How you can use this data

If you’re a business 

You can use this data to:

  • prevent and detect crime, including for:
    • the apprehension or prosecution of alleged offenders
    • proceedings in a court or tribunal
    • complying with an order, a court or a tribunal
  • keep records about land and properties

If you’re a public body

You can use this data if you have a:

  • Public Sector Mapping Agreement Licence
  • One Scotland Mapping Agreement Member Licence

These licences are issued by Ordnance survey and public bodies must use the data under the terms of these licences.

Apply for a licence from Ordnance Survey.

Not sure if you can use this data?

Contact Royal Mail before you use this data. Ask them to confirm that your intended use is permitted under the terms of their licence.

Email Royal Mail.
