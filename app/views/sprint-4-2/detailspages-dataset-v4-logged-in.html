Registered Leases Data: about this dataset

Page contents:

  • About this licence
  • Registered leases dataset details
  • What the dataset looks like

The Registered Leases dataset comprises a number of data elements relating to leasehold entries on HM Land Registry’s register.

Data captured includes: lease date, the duration or term of the lease and parties to the lease, and also noted is a registered lease against the landlord’s title, providing this is registered.
