  • every address in England and Wales, create an account

What will I get if I create an account?

This is a new service and it is still being built. This means that you will not see all licensed data files once you have an account. HM Land Registry expects to have moved every licensed data file to the service by DD/MM/YY.

Account-holders can currently see 3 data files:

  • UK companies that own property in England and Wales
  • Overseas companies that own property in England and Wales
  • registered leases

If you are looking for other data files, you can see a list of the other data files from HM Land Registry and where to find them.

Why you need an account

HM Land Registry asks you to create an account so we can:

  • record who has agreed to the data licence
  • ask you for information so we can check your identity
  • prevent data fraud

We check your identity to prevent data fraud. Licensed data can only be used in a certain way (according to the licence terms) and if you use the data outside the licence you can be taken to court.

How long it takes before you can get the data

It takes about 5 minutes to apply for your account, but HM Land Registry can take a further 2 working days to approve it. This means that you may need to wait 2 working days before you can get the data.

It takes 2 days because HM Land Registry needs to check your identity before you can see the data. In most cases, we can do this quickly and you can download the data on the same day you create your account.

Check what you can do with the data before you create an account

HM Land Registry recommends that you check the data licences before you create an account. The licences explain what you can do with the data.

  • [LINK: Check the licence for UK companies that own property in England and Wales]
  • [LINK: Check the licence for Overseas companies that own property in England and Wales]

If you are not sure what you can do with the data, check with Royal Mail. Royal Mail owns some of the data in these files. They can tell you how you can use the data. example@royalmail.com

Information you need to open an account

To create an account, you need to tell HM Land Registry your:

  • name
  • email
  • address
  • phone number
  • country of residence

If you’re using the data for work, you’ll also need to include information about the organisation you work for.

If your office is in the UK, this will be your:

  • company name
  • office address
  • type of company
  • company registration number

If your office is outside the UK, this will be the:

  • company name
  • office address
  • country where your organisation is registered

You will also need to tell us your credit/debit card details. You will not be charged and no payment will be taken. We ask for your debit/credit card details to check your identity.
